Welcome to Papyri.info

Image:   HGV: P.Ryl. 2 65 — (March 1st, 67 BCE) The grave-diggers Petosiris & Paris are fined for breaking a grave-diggers guild contract by carrying off corpses which weren't assigned to them.

Papyri.info has two primary components. The Papyrological Navigator (PN) supports searching, browsing, and aggregation of ancient papyrological documents and related materials.

The Papyrological Editor (PE) enables multi-author, version controlled, peer reviewed scholarly curation of papyrological texts, translations, commentary, scholarly metadata, institutional catalog records, bibliography, and images.
