            NN from the Themistos division of the Arsinoite nome.  Marinus acknowledges . . to Theogeiton, son of Theogeiton . . . to have sold to Theogeiton 11 miscellaneous sheep belonging to Marinus, which were acquired through purchase and herded together.  And Theogeiton has taken from Marinus these sheep as they are and irrevocably at a price agreed upon by each of them of 192 silver drachmas, which Marinus is receiving from Theogeiton immediately.  And they guarantee that Marinus, the acknowledging party, is bound by every guarantee to Theogeiton in accordance with terms of this sale until year ? of the Emperor Nero Trajan Augustus Germanicus has passed . . . . I, Marinus, have sold Theogeiton the said 11 sheep, as they are and irrevocably, and I am receiving the sum of 192 drachmas and I will give the guarantee as above.  I, Ptollas son of Heras, have written for him because he writes slowly. I, Marinus, have sold and am receiving the sum and will guarantee as above.  Theogeiton, son of Theogeiton, . . .
